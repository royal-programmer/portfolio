# Objectives

## Primary goals

1. **Single URL, multiple identities** — Present **Engineer**, **Trader**, and **Photographer** personas from one site without feeling like three separate products.
2. **Premium, modern UI** — Glass-style surfaces, fluid motion, readable typography, strong light/dark behavior.
3. **Discoverable easter eggs** — Hidden triggers that reward curiosity (logo hold sequences, optional keyboard shortcut), without blocking normal use.
4. **Low-friction, free-tier deployment** — Frontend on a generous host (e.g. Vercel); optional free backend later (e.g. Supabase) for contact forms and dynamic content.
5. **Honest, maintainable scope** — Start with a solid shell and placeholder content; evolve sections as real copy, projects, and media arrive.

## Target audiences

- **Recruiters / hiring managers** — Quick scan of engineering depth, projects, and contact.
- **Collaborators** — Clear sense of skills and how to reach you.
- **Personal brand** — Cohesive story across tech, markets, and visual work (where you choose to expose each).

## Success criteria (MVP)

- [ ] Live URL with HTTPS and acceptable Lighthouse scores on mobile.
- [ ] Engineer persona shows **real** projects (not only placeholders).
- [ ] Contact path works (form or clear mailto) with basic spam considerations.
- [ ] Trader and photographer sections match what you are comfortable publishing publicly (including disclaimers where needed).

## Non-goals (for now)

- Full trading diary with live P&L or brokerage integration.
- Heavy CMS unless content volume justifies it.
- Perfect pixel parity across every browser edge case before launch.
